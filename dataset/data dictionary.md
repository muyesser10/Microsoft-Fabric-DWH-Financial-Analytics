Dataset Data Dictionary
Overview

This dataset contains export financing authorizations issued by an export credit agency.
Each row represents a financing decision related to export transactions.

The dataset includes information about:

financing decisions
exporters and borrowers
financial exposure
geographic information
loan details
export product classifications

Column Definitions

Fiscal Year

Fiscal reporting year in which the financing authorization was issued.


Unique Identifier

Unique identifier assigned to each authorization record.


Deal Number

Identifier representing the financing deal or agreement.


Decision

Indicates the outcome of the financing request.


Decision Date

Date when the financing decision was made.


Effective Date

Date when the financing agreement becomes effective.


Expiration Date

Date when the authorization or financing agreement expires.


Brokered

Indicates whether the financing deal was brokered through an intermediary.


Deal Cancelled

Indicates whether the financing deal was later cancelled after approval.


Country

Country associated with the export transaction.


Program

Export financing program under which the authorization was issued.


Policy Type

Type of financing policy or insurance product used in the transaction.


Decision Authority

Indicates the organizational authority responsible for approving the financing decision.


Primary Export Product NAICS/SIC code

Industry classification code representing the exported product.

These codes follow:

NAICS (North American Industry Classification System)

SIC (Standard Industrial Classification)


Product Description

Textual description of the exported product.


Term

Loan or financing duration.


Primary Applicant

The entity that applied for the export financing.


Primary Lender

Financial institution providing the loan associated with the export transaction.


Primary Exporter

Company responsible for exporting the goods.


Primary Exporter City

City where the exporting company is located.


Primary Exporter State Code

State abbreviation representing the location of the exporter.


Primary Exporter State Name

Full name of the state where the exporter is located.


Primary Borrower

Entity receiving the financing or loan.



Primary Source of Repayment (PSOR)

Identifies the primary source of loan repayment.



Working Capital Delegated Authority

Indicates whether the financing was approved under delegated authority rules for working capital programs.


Approved/Declined Amount

Total amount requested and approved (or declined) for the financing deal.



Disbursed/Shipped Amount

Amount that has actually been disbursed or shipped under the financing agreement.


Undisbursed Exposure Amount

Amount that has been approved but not yet disbursed.


Outstanding Exposure Amount

Remaining financial exposure currently outstanding.



Small Business Authorized Amount

Portion of the authorization amount attributed to small business exporters.


Woman Owned Authorized Amount

Amount associated with woman-owned businesses participating in the transaction.


Minority Owned Authorized Amount

Amount associated with minority-owned businesses involved in the export transaction.


Loan Interest Rate

Interest rate applied to the financing agreement.



Multiyear Working Capital Extension

Indicates whether the working capital authorization includes a multi-year extension.
